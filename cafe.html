Cafe Hoppe

Fancy a night out with friends or just to meet new people? Then Café Hoppe is the place to be. One of the most popular cafés in Amsterdam located on the Spui since 1670. Café Hoppe has something unique, something indescribable, that speaks to the imagination or many-young and old-who come and come back again. Stop by for a drink and enjoy the atmosphere in Café Hoppe. Did you know that: Hans van Mierlo laid the foundations for the liberal D66 party here, and our recently abdicated Queen Beatrix honored us with a royal visit while still a young princess.
